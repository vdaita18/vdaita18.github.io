This form of investment is high-risk, high-gain and should usually be used for long term money gain. When you buy a stock, you are buying a share, or portion, of the company. A mutual fund is a type of investment in which investors pool their money together to buy a portfolio of stocks, bonds or other securities in order to take advantage of diversification and professional portfolio management at a reasonable cost. It is essentially you outsourcing your money to an investor, who diversifies your money through various different forms. This is a medium-risk with medium growth investing option.

Unlike stocks and mutual funds, bonds are a contractual obligation with an issuer that requires them to pay you, otherwise, they are legally in default.
