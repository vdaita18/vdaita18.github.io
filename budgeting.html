What is a budget?

A budget is a detailed plan of costs and streams of revenue typically designed for a monthly plan. It is a critical aspect of your financial plan and allows you to make sure that you are not straying away from your monthly limit. This will be the centerpiece of your finances, going hand in hand with your credit/debit card plan, your monthly income, and your bank accounts. They will all merge into your budget, keeping you free of worry of how to handle your money.
